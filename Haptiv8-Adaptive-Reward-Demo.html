Adaptive reward engine — live demo

Engagement decays as the child habituates to a reward. When it falls past E_low and the minimum dwell time has passed, the engine switches to the next permitted reward and engagement recovers — the decline–switch–recover loop from the report. The sensory and device constraints filter which rewards are allowed.
